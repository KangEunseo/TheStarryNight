Landscape Image

This is a very basic example of a fluid image that has a landscape orientation.

Considerations

  • Loading in massive images for mobile screens isn't a good idea from a performance standpoint. Load a small image by default.
  • Content in the large landscape image may not be legible on small screens. This post demonstrates how a tighter crop might be required for smaller screens.
  • Avoid text in images, especially for fluid images whose size are subject to change.
